Animals

Learn about arctic animals and their environment with these free arctic animal flashcards! Use the arctic background to explain where you might find the animals and what they might eat. These teaching materials work especially well when teaching food chains!

Free

Letters

All alphabet coloring sheets contain the uppercase and lowercase of the letters A to Z. Firstly, children can color in the letter, helping to improve their grip and fine motor skills.

Colors

Teach colors in multiple ways with these versatile color flashcards. As usual, the flashcards come in different sets offering maximum customization for teachers and parents alike.

Shapes

Teach shapes easily with these fantastic shape flashcards! As usual, the flashcards come in more than one version, offering flexible customization for teachers and parents.

Numbers

Teach numbers 1 to 100, counting, math or skip counting with these number flashcards! Play matching, ordering, grouping, and math games. Watch your classroom come alive!
